Marketing and promotion is the key to building a business. The traditional marketing process involves publishing ads through newspapers, television, telephone, radio, brochures, notices etc. But with the advancement in technology and with the rise in internet usage, marketing methods got a new form; it evolved into digital marketing.

What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing involves marketing products and services through the internet with the help of electronic devices. The main channels involved in digital marketing are search engines, social media & other websites, emails etc. This method provides a new way of understanding how customers behave and in the promotion of products and services at the right place, at the right time to the right audience. So endless possibilities are provided for all brands to market themselves in a better way.

Types of Digital Marketing

Content Marketing

Content marketing includes creating, publishing and sharing content to attract the audience. Content is usually published on websites and then promoted through social media, campaigns, SEO etc. Blogs, e-books, infographics etc helps in facilitating content marketing.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Optimizing your websites to rank higher in search results is important to promote your business. SEO techniques such as on-page optimization, off-page optimization, keyword search etc will increase the visibility of a website.

Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing is aimed to increase brand awareness and to get leads. This strategy is carried out by posting campaigns, blogs and sharing links through social media channels like Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, twitter etc. Youtube marketing is also a part of social media marketing.

Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising

This strategy will help you to reach the audience through paid ads. You can set up PPC campaigns and ads on search engines, social media etc and direct people towards your brand through them.

Other popular strategies of digital marketing are video blogging, email marketing, SMS marketing, web design and development, affiliate marketing etc.

The Scope of Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is vital for building a brand, maintaining it and promoting it, especially in this technological age. For example, every brand is required to have a website, so at least they have a social media presence and customers will get a chance to know more about them by visiting the websites. A website will increase the chance of attracting more customers towards your business. The customers will rely on the website content to know more about the brands, products and services.

By posting ads and campaigns on social media, you can target a certain section of people, whom you prefer to make your customers. You can make your ads highly specific and well defined to attract the right audience. Similarly, you can experiment with a variety of marketing techniques within your budget to attract more people. Digital marketing has so many strategies associated with it.

Digital marketing has created a wide area of job opportunities and startups. The scope of digital marketing is gradually increasing. It can take all the products and services to the global level. The recent years have seen significant growth in this field. So, learning a digital marketing course will definitely provide better career options in the present day, as well as for the future. New startups are focusing on digital marketing firms to boost up their productivity. So there is no doubt that you will be having higher salaries and secured job profiles in the field of digital marketing.
